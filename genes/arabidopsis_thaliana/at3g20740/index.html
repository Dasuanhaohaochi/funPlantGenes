Germplasm Phenotype:

  • CS71712  — Embryo lethal.
  • CS73242  — female gametophytic lethal; 50% seed abortion in heterozygous plants
  • CS8172  — Autonomous seed development is initiated in absence of fertilization, with a penetrance of about 77%; autonomous seeds do not contain an embryo but only endosperm; autonomous endosperm development is accompanied by increase in pistil elongation and ovule size, leading to a small developing seed that contains endosperm. Abnormal development of the endosperm posterior pole with a cyst and nodules larger than in the wild type; ectopic location of nodules in the peripheral endosperm; absence of cellularisation and overproliferation at late stages. Gametophytic maternal effect results in embryo lethality.
  • fie  — Initiation of seed development in absence of fertilization
